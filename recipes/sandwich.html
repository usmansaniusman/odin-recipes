Sandwich

Description

The ultimate quick-and-easy everyday sandwich. Thinly sliced deli ham and sliced cheddar cheese are paired with crisp lettuce and a light spread of mustard and mayo between two slices of soft sandwich bread.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white or whole wheat sandwich bread
  • 3–4 slices of deli ham
  • 1–2 slices of cheddar cheese (or American cheese)
  • 2 leaves of crisp iceberg or romaine lettuce
  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon yellow or Dijon mustard
  • Butter (optional, for spreading on the outer crusts)

Steps

  1. Lay the two slices of bread flat on a clean cutting board or plate.
  2. Spread mayonnaise evenly on one slice of bread and mustard on the other slice.
  3. Place the cheese slices onto the bottom piece of bread.
  4. Layer the sliced ham evenly over the cheese.
  5. Top the ham with the clean, dry lettuce leaves.
  6. Place the second slice of bread on top to close the sandwich.
  7. Slice diagonally down the middle with a sharp knife and serve.
