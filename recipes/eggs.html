Simple Scrambled Eggs

Description

Soft, fluffy scrambled eggs cooked gently in butter. A quick protein-packed meal that comes together in less than 5 minutes on the stove.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon milk or water
  • 1 teaspoon butter
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of black pepper
  1. Crack the eggs into a small bowl, add the milk, salt, and pepper, and whisk with a fork until smooth.
  2. Melt the butter in a non-stick skillet over medium-low heat.
  3. Pour in the egg mixture and let it sit for a few seconds until the edges start to set.
  4. Gently push the eggs across the pan with a spatula to form soft curds.
  5. Remove the pan from heat just before the eggs are fully set, as they will continue cooking from the pan's heat, then serve immediately.
