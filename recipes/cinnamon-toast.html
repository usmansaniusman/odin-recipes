Basic Cinnamon Toarst

Description

Crispy toasted bread brushed with melted butter and topped with a sweet, fragrant layer of cinnamon sugar. Simple, warm, and comforting.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white or wheat bread
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 teaspoon granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Mix the sugar and ground cinnamon together in a small bowl or cup.
  2. Toast the slices of bread in a toaster until golden brown and firm.
  3. Spread the softened butter evenly over one side of each warm toast slice.
  4. Sprinkle the cinnamon-sugar mixture generously over the buttered toast while still hot.
  5. Slice in half and serve warm.
